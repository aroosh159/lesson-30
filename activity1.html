Bala Hatun

Rabia Bala Hatun was the wife of Osman I, the founder of the Ottoman Dynasty. She was the daughter of Sheikh Edebali and the mother of Alaeddin Ali Pasha of the Ottoman Empire. See Projects

Fatma Melek Hatun was the first Ottoman princess, the only daughter of Osman Ghazi. She was born in 1284, Söğüt.
